Dress Code

"Think elegant garden party meets Mexican hacienda — dressed to dance under the stars."

We kindly ask all guests to adhere to a formal dress code to help us create a magical and cohesive atmosphere.

👗 Ladies
Do wear

Floor-length gowns in any color — florals, jewel tones, and earth tones are especially welcome.

Please avoid
  • Cocktail dresses or anything above the knee
  • Red in any shade
  • Sequins or heavy embellishment
  • White, ivory, or cream

👠 The ceremony is partially outdoors — block heels or flats are highly recommended for the garden.

🤵 Gentlemen
Do wear

Dark suits or tuxedos.

Please avoid
  • Casual suits or sport coats
  • Jeans, sneakers, or open-toed shoes

🌿 Light linen suits are acceptable given the warm Mexican climate.
