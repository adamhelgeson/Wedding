Dress Code

"Think elegant garden party meets Mexican hacienda — dressed to dance under the stars."

We kindly ask all guests to adhere to a formal dress code to help us create a magical and cohesive atmosphere.

👗 Ladies
Do wear

Floor-length gowns in any color — florals, jewel tones, and earth tones are especially welcome.

Please avoid
  • Cocktail dresses or anything above the knee
  • Red in any shade
  • Sequins or heavy embellishment
  • Sheer fabrics, see-through elements, or corset-style bodices
  • White, ivory, or cream

👠 The entire evening is outdoors — block heels, flats, or clean dressy sneakers are recommended for the vineyard and garden. A light wrap is also suggested for the cooler evening air.

🤵 Gentlemen
Do wear

Dark suits or tuxedos with formal shoes or clean dressy sneakers.

Please avoid
  • Casual suits or sport coats
  • Jeans
  • Light-colored suits
  • Open-toed shoes

💟 The entire evening is outdoors — clean dressy sneakers are acceptable given the vineyard and garden setting.
